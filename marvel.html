Marvel Characters

Iron Man

Real name: Tony Stark

Powers: Genius-level intelligence, advanced technology suit

Billionaire industrialist and genius inventor, Tony Stark (Robert Downey Jr.), is kidnapped and forced to build a devastating weapon. Instead, using his intelligence and ingenuity, Tony builds a high-tech suit of armor and escapes captivity. When he uncovers a nefarious plot with global implications, he dons his powerful armor and vows to protect the world as Iron Man.

Captain America

Real name: Steve Rogers

Powers: Super strength, agility, and endurance

Steve Rogers (Chris Evans) volunteers to participate in an experimental program that turns him into the super soldier Captain America. As Captain America, Rogers joins forces with Bucky Barnes (Sebastian Stan) and Peggy Carter (Hayley Atwell) to wage war on the evil HYDRA organization, led by the villainous Red Skull (Hugo Weaving).

Black Widow

Real name: Natasha Romanoff

Powers: Peak human physical condition, highly trained in martial arts and espionage

Natasha Romanoff (Scarlett Johansson), also known as Black Widow, is a skilled assassin and former agent of the Soviet agency for foreign intelligence, the KGB. She defies her programming and defects to the United States, where she becomes a member of the Avengers and uses her cunning and expertise to defend the world from threats large and small.

Thor

Real name: Thor Odinson

Powers: Super strength, agility, and endurance, control of the elements

Thor (Chris Hemsworth) is the crown prince of Asgard, an advanced alien civilization with highly advanced technology and powerful magical abilities. When his reckless actions reignite an ancient war, Thor is cast out of Asgard and sent to live amongst humans on Earth. Stripped of his powers and his mighty hammer, Mjolnir, Thor must learn humility and become a true hero in order to reclaim his place in Asgard and prevent the destruction of his homeland.

Hulk

Real name: Bruce Banner

Powers: Super strength, durability, and regenerative powers

Dr. Bruce Banner (Mark Ruffalo) is a brilliant scientist who, due to a freak accident, becomes the incredibly powerful and destructive monster known as the Hulk. As the Hulk, Banner struggles to control his rage and prevent his inner beast from causing destruction and chaos. He must also contend with his arch-nemesis, the evil General Thunderbolt Ross (William Hurt), who seeks to capture and exploit the Hulk's powers for his own gain.
